About Me

Oh, hey! I didn't see you there friend... and future coffee break buddy? If you couldn't tell from my name overwhelming your eye balls just now, my name is Mark Holmes. I'm from Chicago, born and raised, and moved to Seattle in 2016 to be closer to the elements and start a family.

I approach creative challenges with a clean slate, open minded and ready to connect clients with their intended audiences. In the past 8 years I've worked for both B2C and B2B companies producing both web and print products. I've developed brands identities both large and small.

I'm out to create simple solutions to complex problems with beautiful aesthetics.
